If $y=r^3/3$ then the differential of $y$ $(dy)$ is what?

$$
\begin{align}
  y &=\frac{1}{3}\ r^3 \\
  \therefore \frac{dy}{dr}\ &= \frac{1}{3} 3 r^{3-1} \\
  \frac{dy}{dx} &=r^2 \\
  \therefore dy &=r^2\cdot dr=r\cdot dr\cdot r
\end{align}
$$
